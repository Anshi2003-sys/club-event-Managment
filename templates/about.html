About Campus Connect

VibeHub is a platform designed to bring campus events and clubs together. Our goal is to provide students with easy access to all events, clubs, and activities happening on campus, making student life more engaging and interactive.

From cultural fests to tech workshops, VibeHub ensures you never miss out on an exciting opportunity to connect, learn, and grow with your peers.
